# Electrician's Tool Set

| **Tool**                                | **Description**                                                               | **Purpose**                                                          |
|-----------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Wire Strippers**                      | Used to strip the insulation off electrical wires.                            | Essential for preparing wires for connections.                       |
| **Pliers (Needle Nose)**                | Pliers with long, thin jaws to reach tight spaces.                            | For gripping, bending, and cutting wires.                            |
| **Insulated Screwdrivers**              | Screwdrivers with insulated handles to prevent shocks.                        | For turning screws on outlets, switches, and junction boxes.         |
| **Voltage Tester**                      | A tool used to detect the presence of voltage in a circuit.                   | To check if wires or circuits are live.                              |
| **Multimeter**                          | A device used to measure voltage, current, and resistance.                    | For testing and troubleshooting electrical systems.                  |
| **Fish Tape**                           | A tool for pushing or pulling wires through conduits.                         | To guide wire through walls or conduits.                             |
| **Cordless Drill**                      | A power drill used for drilling holes and driving screws.                     | For drilling holes in walls and installing screws.                   |
| **Conduit Bender**                      | Tool used to bend conduit to required angles.                                 | To shape metal conduit to fit around obstacles.                      |
| **Hammer**                              | A standard hammer for driving nails and securing materials.                   | To secure junction boxes and other fittings.                         |
| **Utility Knife**                       | A sharp knife used to cut insulation and other materials.                     | For cutting wire insulation and other materials.                     |
| **Cutting Tools (Cable Cutter)**        | Used to cut electrical wires and cables.                                      | To cut wire to desired lengths.                                      |
| **Fish Tapes (with light)**             | Fish tape with a light attachment to help with wire placement.                | For pushing wire through long or hard-to-reach places.               |
| **Circuit Finder**                      | A device used to detect which breaker controls a particular circuit.          | Helps identify which circuit breakers correspond to which outlets.   |
| **GFCI Outlet Tester**                  | A tester that checks the functionality of GFCI outlets.                       | To verify the safety and proper operation of GFCI outlets.           |
| **Tape Measure**                        | A measuring tool used for accurate measurements.                              | For measuring lengths of wire or spaces for electrical fixtures.     |
| **Level**                               | A tool used to ensure electrical outlets and fixtures are installed straight. | Ensures proper alignment of outlets and fixtures.                    |
| **Crimping Tool**                       | A tool used to attach connectors to wire ends.                                | For securing connectors to wire for a proper electrical connection.  |
| **Flashlight or Headlamp**              | A portable light source for working in dark spaces.                           | To illuminate dark areas, especially in attics, basements, or walls. |
| **Tool Belt**                           | A belt with pouches and holsters for carrying tools.                          | Keeps tools organized and easily accessible.                         |
| **Socket Wrenches**                     | A set of wrenches with interchangeable sockets.                               | For tightening and loosening nuts and bolts.                         |
| **Soldering Iron**                      | A tool used for melting solder to join electrical components.                 | For making precise electrical connections.                           |
| **Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)** | Includes gloves, goggles, hard hat, etc.                                      | To ensure safety from electrical hazards and debris.                 |
